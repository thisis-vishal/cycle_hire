What is Bicycle Hire?


Bicycle hire is an idea to make travel easy in
University Premises You can simply hire a bicycle
and use it to travel inside the campus and whenever
you done with bike you can return it by following simple steps






Why Bicycle Hire?

.

Due to big campus area many student feel difficult
to travel in campus and in harsh weather it become
more difficult ot travel form one location to another
there for Team AVSR came with an idea of cycle hire
